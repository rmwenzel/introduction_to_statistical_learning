b. Calculating PVE by hand

Confusingly the “components” in sklearn are what the book calls principal component loading vectors, in this case ϕ1,…,ϕ4\phi_1, \dots, \phi_4ϕ1​,…,ϕ4​ (see table 10.1). They are given as the loading matrix ϕ=[ϕ1,…,ϕ4]⊤\phi = [\phi_1, \dots, \phi_4]^\topϕ=[ϕ1​,…,ϕ4​]⊤
